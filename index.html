Welcome!

I am Sumedh.

I am a rising senior at the Georgia Institute of Technology, studying Computer Science, with a concentration in Intelligence and Info-Internetworks (AI and big data, in a nutshell). I am particularly interested in and passionate about artificial intelligence and its potential to improve people's lives, particularly in smart cities, transportation, and, to an extent, healthcare. I have attended numerous hackathons from Atlanta to Los Angeles, and these experiences have helped me hone my problem-solving, ideation, and technical skills. Outside of software and school, I am an avid electronic dance music fan, and have been producing music for 4 years and DJing for almost 2 years. You can listen to my entire discography in the widget on the right!

In Spring 2021, I put together a virtual music festival including 3 Georgia Tech-based acts. This led me to sign up to be the new president of Art Matters, and I will update this page with more experiences and skills learned from that experience in Fall 2021.

I am a Senior Teaching Assistant of GT's Objects and Design course, or CS 2340. I created a website for course-related study materials called obdes over the summer, and am constantly improving it along with my amazing team! I am involved in the GT Quiz Bowl team, the RoboCup (robotic soccer) team, and Bits of Good, an organization that creates web applications for non-profits! I am also a part of the new ACT Driving Simulation Vertically Integrated Project, and I am working on autonomous vehicle trajectory control using reinforcement learning!

I will be interning at Bank of America as a Software Engineering Intern this summer in the Global Technology department, so I am NOT LOOKING FOR EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITIES AT THE MOMENT. However, I am always interested in discussing startup or project ideas, and I'm willing to join any project that piques my interest if it meshes with my ethos of doing more good than harm!
